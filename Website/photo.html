What Is Photo

    A Photo Resistor is a type of resistor made from semiconductor material with high resistance. It reacts to light by lowering its resistance as the amount of light increases. This occurs because the photons from the light give energy to the electrons within the semiconductor, causing them to jump from their bound state to the conduction band and conduct electricity, which in turn lowers the resistance.

Why Is It Important

    A photoresistor is a really cool type of sensor because it can detect changes in light levels. This is important for lots of things, like making sure your automatic lights turn on when it gets dark, adjusting camera settings for better pictures, and helping solar panels work more efficiently. By using a photoresistor, you can make sure that your device responds to changes in light levels, which can be super helpful in lots of different situations.

Ideal Values

    The ideal value for the photoresistor in a composting bin depends on the amount of sunlight present, as this affects the light levels that the photoresistor will be detecting.
