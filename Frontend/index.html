What is Forest Sense?

Forests are vital to biodiversity, climate regulation, and overall ecological balance. To protect these invaluable ecosystems, I created Forest Sense – a novel system designed to revolutionize the way we monitor and safeguard our forests. Forest Sense aims to establish a mesh network of nodes around a forest area. The nodes are powered by ESP32s and ESP32CAM microcontrollers and are used to deploy various sensors depending on the functionality of the node. This network will be integrated with a backend system to store real-time data and transmit to ForestSense Website. The system collects information that is used to detect the presence of a fire in the forest and alert the relevant authorities. Additionally, the system uses machine learning to detect the presence of a fire in a forest by analyzing real-time pictures captured by the camera node.

Forest Sense Approach

Forest fires are mostly caused by human activity, accounting for 90% of all forest fires. This includes unattended campfires, burning debris, and arson. It's crucial for individuals to take necessary precautions to prevent forest fires from occurring and be mindful of their impact on the environment. By doing so, I can help reduce the number of human-caused forest fires and protect our natural resources.
